KILL BILL | ===> SZA <===

The vengeful song revolves around the themes of betrayal and the desire for revenge. It delves into the emotions of a person who has been cheated on by their ex and is still in love with them.

The lyrics describe the homicidal tendencies of the speaker, who wants to kill their ex and their new partner, rather than being alone. It explores the internal conflict of being mad and hurt, but still attached to the person who broke their heart.
