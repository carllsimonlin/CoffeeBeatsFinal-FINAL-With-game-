YAKAP | ===> ZACK TABUDLO <===

The romantic song revolves around the themes of confession and affection. It delves into the emotions of a person who has been in love with someone for a long time and is finally ready to express their feelings.

The lyrics describe the nervousness and excitement of the speaker, who wants to hug their crush tightly and tell them how much they mean to them. It explores the internal conflict of being shy and hesitant, but also brave and hopeful.
