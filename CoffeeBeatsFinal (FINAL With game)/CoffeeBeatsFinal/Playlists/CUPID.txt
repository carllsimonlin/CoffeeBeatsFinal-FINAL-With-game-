CUPID | ===> FIFTY FIFTY <===

The catchy song revolves around the themes of loneliness and the desire for love. It delves into the emotions of a person who has been single for a long time and is looking for someone to share their feelings with.

The lyrics describe the frustration of waiting for Cupid to shoot his arrows and find a match for them, someone who can make them happy and fulfilled. It explores the internal conflict of being hopeless and skeptical about love, but still wanting it more and more.
