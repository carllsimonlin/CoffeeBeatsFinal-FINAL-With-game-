COMETHRU | ===> JEREMY ZUCKER <===

This heartfelt song revolves around the themes of vulnerability and the fear of letting someone in. It delves into the emotions of a person who has built walls around their heart due to past experiences.

The lyrics describe the struggle of finding someone who is worth breaking down those barriers for, someone who can bring light into your life and make you feel truly alive. It explores the internal struggle of wanting to let someone in, but being afraid of getting hurt again.
