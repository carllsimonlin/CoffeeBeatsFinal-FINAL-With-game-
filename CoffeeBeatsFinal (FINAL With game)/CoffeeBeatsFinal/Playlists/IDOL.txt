IDOL | ===> YOASOBI <===

The ironic song revolves around the themes of deception and perfection. It delves into the emotions of an idol, who is seen as flawless and invincible by the media and the fans.

The lyrics describe the secrets and lies that the idol hides behind their smile, while they pretend to be happy and innocent. It explores the internal conflict of being loved by everyone, but not being able to love anyone.
