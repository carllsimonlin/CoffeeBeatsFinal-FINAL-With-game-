DECALCOMANIE | ===> MAMAMOO <===

The energetic song revolves around the themes of attraction and tension. It delves into the emotions of a woman who is drawn to a mysterious man, despite knowing that he is dangerous.

The lyrics describe the suspense and excitement of the speaker, who wants to kiss him, but also fears crossing the line. It explores the internal conflict of being sober and cautious, but also drunk and reckless.
