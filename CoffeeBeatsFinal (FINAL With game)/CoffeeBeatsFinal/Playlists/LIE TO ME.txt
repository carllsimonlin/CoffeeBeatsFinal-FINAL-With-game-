LIE TO ME | ===> 5SOS <===

The emotional song revolves around the themes of betrayal and heartbreak. It delves into the emotions of a person who has been cheated on by their ex and is still in love with them.

The lyrics describe the pain and jealousy of the speaker, who sees their ex looking happy with someone else. It explores the internal conflict of being angry and hurt, but also attached and hopeful.
