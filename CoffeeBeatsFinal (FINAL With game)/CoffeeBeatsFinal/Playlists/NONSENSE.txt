NONSENSE | ===> SABRINA CARPENTER <===

The humorous song revolves around the themes of infatuation and the loss of words. It delves into the emotions of a person who is so smitten by their crush that they start talking nonsense whenever they are around them.

The lyrics describe the awkward and funny situations that the speaker finds themselves in, such as forgetting their ex, losing their chill, and jumping the octave. It explores the internal conflict of being honest and confident, but also nervous and silly.
