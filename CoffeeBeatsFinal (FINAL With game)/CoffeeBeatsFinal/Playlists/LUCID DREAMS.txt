LUCID DREAMS | ==> JUICE WRLD <===

The heartbreaking song revolves around the themes of loss and regret. It delves into the emotions of a person who has been dumped by their ex and is still in love with them.

The lyrics describe the agony and anger of the speaker, who sees their ex moving on with someone else. It explores the internal conflict of being in love and in hate, but also unable to change or forget.
