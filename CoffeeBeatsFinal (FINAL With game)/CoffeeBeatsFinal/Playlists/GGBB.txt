GGBB | ===> MAMAMOO <===

The playful song revolves around the themes of attraction and contradiction. It delves into the emotions of a good girl who falls for a bad boy, despite knowing that he is trouble.

The lyrics describe the dilemma and confusion of the speaker, who wants to be with him, but also fears getting hurt. It explores the internal conflict of being sweet and nice, but also spicy and naughty.
